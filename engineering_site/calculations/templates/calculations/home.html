How to use

  • Select a calculator and provide realistic conveyor design parameters.
  • Use consistent units. Throughput expects tonnes per hour, and lift height requires metres.
  • The belt tension calculator leverages Euler's belt friction equation.
  • Document results for your design workbook or export them to spreadsheets.

Calculations are simplified and should be validated against project-specific standards such as CEMA or ISO 5048.
